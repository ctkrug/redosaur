ReDoSaur

Paste a regex. Watch it explode.

ReDoSaur runs your pattern through an actual instrumented backtracking engine — not a heuristic linter — counts every real backtracking step, generates a genuine worst-case input, and shows the exponential blowup as a live counter. Then it suggests a fix and proves the counter stops.
